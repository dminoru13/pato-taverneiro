O que é rpg ?

The tabletop RPG is basically a game where players roleplay their characters as if they were actors who play a character in a film, soap opera or theater, with the The difference is that in tabletop RPGs there is no script for the character to follow, he is free to make your decisions and make your choices

HOW TO PLAY

-THE PLAYERS-
The most famous RPGs were shaped for a group of 4 to 6 players, where one of these players will be the Narrator of the adventure to be told.
Players who are not the Storyteller will create and control a Character, which will be created based on the rules
╬╬═════════❮◆❯═════════╬╬


-NARRATOR/MASTER-
the Narrator will be the one who will control the world around the Characters, like the director of a piece, it will describe locations, creatures and all other elements that the Characters will encounter during the adventure.

The Narrator is part of everything in the adventure, responsible for create combats and also creatures, request tests to players face challenges in the world (such as jumping over a wall, climbing something, for example) in addition to play the NPCs (Non-Playable Characters) of that campaign, as peasants, guards, kings and tavern keepers, why not?
