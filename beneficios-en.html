7 benefits of playing Tabletop RPG
╬╬═════════❮◆❯═════════╬╬

It is increasingly clear that Tabletop RPG has several benefits beyond just entertain. Table RPG is increasingly becoming an ally for developing skills socio-emotional, behavioral and cognitive aspects of children, young people and adults.

It is possible to observe, especially in young people, that RPG brings a purpose to the study of a given scenario, environment and even strategy. In my RPG day-to-day life, I see young people starting to study biology to understand monsters, astronomy to learn more about planets and space travel in your games, math and physics to simulate understanding of engineering your characters and so on.


Furthermore, RPG brings more benefits than just generating interest and purpose. We will see?

Cognitive Capacity
╬╬═════════❮◆❯═════════╬╬

The ability to acquire knowledge through perception, attention, memory, reasoning, imagination, thought and language is, in Tabletop RPG, an attribute that expands considerably. Because, the stimuli for master and players are intense during a RPG campaign.

Players receive a flood of information to understand a new universe that will be studied and explored through its characters, without counting on system information they are playing.

As a mental tool, RPG also stimulates the imagination that works the process creative and emotional. This way the player is in constant active stimulation! Opposite of who just watch a movie or a series, as there is no need to think or imagine, you already receive the stimulus ready. In RPG, you need to imagine everything!

Reading Habit
╬╬═════════❮◆❯═════════╬╬

In a generation where reading is becoming increasingly rare and young people are killing themselves to reading the complex books pushed down our throats by schools becomes more difficult encourage reading. However, through RPG we observed that disinterested young people start a reading process, as well as adults who had forgotten this habit.

For the purpose of understanding a system for playing, reading a rule book, for example, or even reading history books to pick up references and narrative styles, those who play RPG amplifies the habit of reading. Furthermore, we know that reading is an excellent method of stimulating own thinking and imagination.

Therefore, playing RPG can help you get back to your books and read what you really want. he likes! Or encourage your child to do so.

Mathematics
╬╬═════════❮◆❯═════════╬╬

No matter how simple an RPG game is, you know that you will have to do dozens of calculations (mainly in combat) to analyze the results. This way, playing Tabletop RPG initially stimulates basic calculations with the infinity of possibilities in sums, subtractions, multiplications and divisions. Some other rules use percentage, numbers integers and fractions.
Remember that every time you roll a die you will add, multiply, subtract and even divide to calculate the results of your actions. Combining the Habit of Reading with In mathematics we enter a system of text interpretation and logical analysis. That way, you also develop your ability to face and solve problems. Cool huh?

This reasoning ability and speed in doing math has a great impact on the future personal and professional person. Furthermore, calculation and mathematics skills become cream for the person and numbers become great allies.

Interpretation
╬╬═════════❮◆❯═════════╬╬

A skill that can help develop imagination and our social interaction, as We can “play at being” like we used to do. Do you remember wearing clothes superheroes (or villains), playing at being a police officer, a firefighter or an astronaut?

With this role-playing game, the player has the possibility of leaving his sphere of reality and experience the sensations and mentality of a character. Thus, the player can experience the feeling of courage, willpower and even determination that your character expresses.

Furthermore, when playing an RPG character, you have the possibility to face some basic monsters that scare us on a daily basis. For example, the fear of exposure, judgment and criticism.

Another important point is that in the interpretation we can also include the player's decisions. At the same time that he needs to “be inside” the character, he also decides his actions agile way. Thus, learning to make quick decisions in life.

Socialization
╬╬═════════❮◆❯═════════╬╬

One of the greatest achievements of RPG is socialization. When playing a Tabletop RPG as a DM or as player you must socialize and talk. This allows people if meet and new friendships, based on a common interest, begin.

Therefore, that person who is more closed and shy gains space to speak and interpret, and the more expansive person who dominates space learns to listen. We teach this as a of the pillars of our RPG Masters Academy, in the area in which the master needs to know to control the players at the table and make EVERYONE shine.

This is certainly one of the main benefits of RPG, in my view. I see that in our Discord server, people start to get to know each other, play together and then even They keep chatting more about life. This is really cool, as it shows the strength of people with the same interest, in the same place they have.

Creativity
╬╬═════════❮◆❯═════════╬╬

Those who play RPG, most of the time, are people who have a high level of creativity. The imaginary world of the RPG opens the doors to the subconscious and the innovative questioning. When creating a character or even an entire universe, you stimulates this power to create.

This means that when preparing the game, you as the master create the entire narrative and elements that are important to players. Furthermore, as a player you prepare and Create your character for a great adventure. During the game you create situations in together with your colleagues and master.

It's a non-stop creation process.

That's why creativity becomes a skill. Being creative is something you develop and it can take you to several areas of your life.

Strategy
╬╬═════════❮◆❯═════════╬╬

Create strategies for physical and verbal combat, and understand what is happening they are part of understanding the fabric of the world. So, you as a player need to study the enemies and challenges you will face to move towards the success of the campaign.

The master himself has to use strategy to create the campaign, but at the same time be able to change the route if your players act out of line.
